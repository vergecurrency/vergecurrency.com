Discord Bot Manual

  1. Display your Verge Discord address: send "@vergebot deposit" to see the Verge address associated with your Discord account.
  2. Deposit Coins: send XVG coins to the address provided.
  3. Check Balance: send "@vergebot balance" and see your balance in the reply.
  4. Send Coins Over Discord: send "@vergebot tip @ ".
    Note: minimum tip amount - 10 XVG.
  5. Withdraw Discord wallet balance: send "@vergebot withdraw " to withdraw your whole wallet balance (minus 2 XVG withdrawal fee) to your Verge address.
  6. Support Verge: tip @vergedev to support the Verge Team!
